# CodeAlpha Task 3 - Data Visualization using Netflix Dataset

## Project Overview
This project focuses on transforming raw Netflix data into meaningful visualizations using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib. The objective is to analyze Netflix content trends and present insights through clear and effective charts.

## Dataset
Dataset: Netflix Titles Dataset

File Used:
- netflix_titles.csv

## Tools and Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook
- VS Code

## Visualizations Created

### 1. Movies vs TV Shows Distribution
Analyzed the distribution of Movies and TV Shows available on Netflix.

### 2. Netflix Content Added Over the Years
Visualized the growth of Netflix content additions over time.

### 3. Top 10 Countries Producing Netflix Content
Identified the countries contributing the most content to Netflix.

### 4. Top 10 Most Common Genres on Netflix
Analyzed the most frequently occurring genres.

### 5. Netflix Content Ratings Distribution
Explored the distribution of content ratings such as TV-MA, TV-14, and PG-13.

### 6. Movie Duration Distribution
Studied the duration patterns of movies available on Netflix.

## Key Insights

- Movies dominate Netflix's content library compared to TV Shows.
- Netflix experienced rapid growth in content additions after 2015.
- The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
- Drama and International content are among the most common genres.
- TV-MA and TV-14 are the most frequent content ratings.
- Most Netflix movies have durations between 80 and 120 minutes.
